Squirrelville

My uni capstone project, were you play as a squirrel who loves acorns and can climb on almost anything!

❮ ❯


I created Squirrelville in my final year of uni along with five other students. Since I was the only student with Unity experience, and also had a huge lead in practical game-dev experience, I was given a lot of responsibility in this project. I am most proud of the unique and robust movement system for the squirrel, and for helping the other programmers and designers rapidly develop their Unity skills - but I'm also incredible proud of the quality of the final experience. You can find more on our website, https://squirrelville.bearlyhumans.com or download the game from our itch.io page.
