Mastermind

A clean Android app of the classic boardgame.

❮ ❯


The challenge for Mastermind was to make a simple but fully finished, polished and released game in as little time as possible, and since I had been fascinated with the classic board game of the same name at the time (so much that I had made an algorithm that could solve them, it made sense to make a digital copy of that. The original goal was to finish the project in a month, and though it ended up taking two I am still very proud of how well it turned out. The game has been on the google play store, but unfortunately the maintenance required to keep it up became to high, so I am releasing it as a web-GL project instead.
