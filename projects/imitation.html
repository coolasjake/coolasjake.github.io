Imitation

An asymetrical multiplayer game of deception.

❮ ❯


I was part of a 2 week game jam run by PirateSoftware in early 2024. The theme was "it's spreading" and so my team decided to make a casual game that centered around putting out wildfires with a firefighting helicopter. In this project, I was the lead game programmer and I alsodesigned many of the visual and audio effects.

You can play the game by downloading it from our itch.io page.
