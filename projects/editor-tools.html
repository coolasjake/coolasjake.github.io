Headshot

A gravity shifting - robot dismembering shooter.

❮ ❯


Headshot is my magnum-opus, a full 3D PvE shooter with two unique features - a smooth and flexible gravity-changing ability, plus level design to compliment it, and enemies with components that can be destroyed to prevent them taking different actions. I have been putting work into this project on and off for at least 5 years, and through it I have become proficient in so many things, including 3D physics and maths, AI systems such as navigation and state-machines, editor scripting, animation, 3D modelling, UI design, audio systems and much more.
